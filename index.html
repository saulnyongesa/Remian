About Remian Diagnostics

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate healthcare and education professionals committed to advancing the skills, knowledge, and professional growth of medical laboratory scientists, technologists, and technicians.

As an accredited CPD provider, our mission is to deliver high-quality, accessible, and evidence-based learning through interactive webinars and digital resources.

Our Mission

To provide accredited CPD and webinars, medical supplies, and Health Professionals Recruitment.

Our Vision

To be the leading hub for professional growth and innovation in medical laboratory science.

Our Objectives

  1. Organize regular webinars on emerging and re-emerging trends in medical laboratory science
  2. Award KMLTTB accredited CPD points to practising laboratory technologists
  3. Develop online learning resources and training modules for continuous medical education
  4. Facilitate research presentations and case study discussions among professionals
  5. Build partnerships with hospitals, learning institutions, and regulatory bodies to improve practice standards
  6. To supply medical equipment and other essentials
  7. To be the industry leader in the recruitment of healthcare professionals
